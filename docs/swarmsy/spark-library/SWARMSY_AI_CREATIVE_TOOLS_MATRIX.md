# SWARMSY AI Creative Tools Matrix

Use vendor-neutral categories. Pick tools based on fit, safety, licensing, and human review.

| Category | What it helps with | Best use case | Risk | Proof/safety note | Human review requirement | How it fits SWARMSY |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| text/copy AI | scripts, captions, prompts | rapid drafts | fabricated claims | cite sources and verify claims | high | messaging velocity with proof checks |
| image generation AI | concepts, scenes, symbols | mood boards and concept art | style theft/IP confusion | ensure original direction and rights | high | visual world exploration |
| video generation AI | short visuals, teasers | campaign launch clips | deepfake/misattribution | disclose AI use where required | high | lore media acceleration |
| music generation AI | motifs, demos, stems | soundtrack ideation | copyright similarity | run originality and licensing review | high | sonic identity prototyping |
| voice generation AI | narration, character lines | controlled character voice | impersonation misuse | no real-person imitation without rights | very high | persona media production |
| animation tools | motion stories | animated shorts/loops | scope creep | keep continuity bible | high | character world continuity |
| website builders | owned hubs | fast one-page launch | misleading pages | legal and claim checks | medium | owned-channel home base |
| game builders | interactive loops | prototype mini-games | manipulative mechanics | transparent rewards | high | engagement systems |
| chatbot builders | FAQ/lore bots | owned community support | hallucinations | scope boundaries and escalation | high | bot staff operations |
| automation tools | workflow triggers | campaign operations | accidental spam | rate limits + approval gates | high | systemized execution |
| scheduling tools | release cadence | multi-channel publishing | overposting | compliance with platform rules | medium | consistent signal rhythm |
| analytics tools | performance insights | optimize campaigns | vanity metric bias | prefer proof-linked metrics | medium | evidence-based iteration |
| design tools | layouts/brand assets | zines, decks, pages | inconsistent identity | style guide enforcement | medium | cohesive visual language |
| 3D/avatar tools | character assets | toys, game avatars | uncanny/derivative design | originality review | high | world embodiment assets |
| AR/filter tools | interactive effects | campaign activations | platform policy issues | test against platform rules | high | experiential storytelling |
| newsletter tools | email ops | retention and drops | spam/privacy failures | opt-in only and unsubscribe clarity | high | owned audience channel |
| community tools | moderation and roles | healthy group ops | abuse or brigading | published community rules | high | quest/fandom coordination |

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- no deepfake misuse
- no impersonation
- no fake celebrity voices
- no stolen styles
- no fake testimonials
- no undisclosed AI where disclosure is required
- check platform/tool licensing before commercial use
