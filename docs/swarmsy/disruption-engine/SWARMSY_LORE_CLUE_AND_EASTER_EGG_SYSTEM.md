# SWARMSY Lore Clue and Easter Egg System

Define how SPARKY creates participatory mystery without fake claims.

## System Components

- **Public clues**: obvious clues visible to all
- **Hidden clues**: subtle details for close observers
- **Recurring symbols**: stable marks that create continuity
- **Campaign codes**: short coded strings tied to drops
- **Collector unlocks**: rewards for decoding/participation
- **QR trails**: linked locations/pages with permission only
- **Phrase trails**: repeated phrase fragments across posts
- **Visual breadcrumbs**: recurring visual fragments
- **Hidden dates**: encoded date hints for timed reveals
- **Numbered drops**: serialized sequence for collectible memory
- **Audience decoding**: intentional solve challenges
- **Proof-safe mystery**: lore is symbolic; claims are factual
- **No fake claims rule**: never invent events, data, press, or traction

## Design Rules

1. Every clue arc has a start, middle, and reveal.
2. Every reveal must trigger a real next action.
3. Mystery cannot require illegal acts or risky behavior.
4. Unlock rewards must be real and deliverable.
5. Publish proof of solved clues and outcomes.

## Template: 3-Day Clue Campaign

- **Day 1**: symbol drop + partial phrase
- **Day 2**: hidden code + audience decode prompt
- **Day 3**: reveal + action (sign-up/buy/show up)

## Template: 7-Day Clue Campaign

- Days 1-2: introduce symbol and campaign code
- Days 3-5: layered clues (visual + phrase + timed post)
- Day 6: final puzzle gate
- Day 7: reveal, unlock, and proof recap

## Template: 30-Day Clue Campaign

- Week 1: world setup and symbol ownership
- Week 2: recurring clue rhythm and audience onboarding
- Week 3: deeper decode mechanics and collector tiers
- Week 4: final reveal, community recognition, archive release

## Template: Product Drop Clue Trail

- Tease product attribute fragments
- Number each clue card
- Unlock full spec at final code
- Publish real stock/timing proof

## Template: Hidden Identity Clue Trail

- Masked persona clues tied to values
- Voice and symbol consistency checks
- Non-deceptive reveal framing (performance art/lore)
- Final reveal with truthful identity boundaries

## Template: Local Activation Clue Trail (legal-safe only)

- Use permitted local partner locations
- Place legal QR/poster/window assets only with consent
- Mirror all clues digitally for accessibility
- End with approved local event or digital reveal

## Safety Floor

- No fake claims, fake urgency, or fake rewards
- No vandalism/trespass instructions
- No harassment, doxxing, brigading, or manipulation
- Replace spam asks with clue-based legal signal strategy
