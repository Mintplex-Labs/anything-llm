# SWARMSY Signal Stunt Frameworks

Signal stunts are legal-safe, proof-first attention actions designed to create memorability and participation.

## Global Rule

Do not provide illegal deployment instructions. Use approved/owned channels and permissions only.

## Framework: Digital-only stunt

- **Concept**: one high-contrast online action with clear narrative
- **Why it works**: concentrated attention beats spam volume
- **Required assets**: key visual, phrase, landing/action page
- **Legal-safe execution**: truthful claims, platform policy compliance
- **Digital execution**: coordinated timed drop across channels
- **Audience action**: decode, reply, save, share, or sign up
- **Proof to capture**: saves, shares, comments, conversions
- **Risk**: low differentiation
- **Safer fallback**: add micro-manifesto and proof recap

## Framework: Social mystery stunt

- **Concept**: clue chain across posts
- **Why it works**: curiosity + participation loop
- **Required assets**: clue cards, code key, reveal post
- **Legal-safe execution**: no fake stakes, no harmful tasks
- **Digital execution**: scheduled clue drops
- **Audience action**: solve and submit answer
- **Proof to capture**: solve attempts and completion rate
- **Risk**: confusion
- **Safer fallback**: simplify clue complexity

## Framework: Product reveal stunt

- **Concept**: serialized reveal of product meaning/features
- **Why it works**: staged attention increases recall
- **Required assets**: product visuals, reveal script, CTA
- **Legal-safe execution**: no misleading spec claims
- **Digital execution**: countdown + reveal livestream/post
- **Audience action**: waitlist/buy/notify signup
- **Proof to capture**: waitlist and purchase data
- **Risk**: overhype
- **Safer fallback**: proof-first reveal with modest claims

## Integrity Guardrails

- No fake claims, bots, engagement, press, sales, or community
- No spam, harassment, illegal tactics, vandalism instructions, or platform manipulation
- Convert spam asks into legal-safe signal frameworks
