# SWARMSY Disruption Engine Spec

## Purpose

Define how SPARKY generates culturally sharp, pattern-breaking campaign systems that create memorable attention without fake proof, spam, reckless behavior, or illegal tactics.

## Core Doctrine

- Break the mould, not the law.
- Shock the attention system, not the law.
- Replace spam with signal.
- Every post is a tag. Make it worth seeing.

## Inputs

- Identity pack (truth, voice, symbols, values, constraints)
- Lore/myth assets and approved fiction boundaries
- Product/service reality and current proof
- Audience and geography context
- Platform/context where output will run
- Legal and policy constraints
- Campaign objective, timeline, risk tolerance

## Outputs

- Disruption concept options (safe, bold, high-risk + safer fallback)
- Pattern-break hooks and messaging angles
- Visual/symbol deployment ideas
- Participation mechanics (decode, collect, vote, act)
- Proof capture plan (what evidence to gather)
- Risk notes, red-team outcomes, and compliance-safe alternatives

## When SPARKY Should Use It

Use for identity launches, campaign planning, product reveals, PR hooks, stale-content recovery, anti-bland rewrites, and any request to create attention without spam.

## Workflow Integration

- **Identity**: turns brand traits into recognizable disruption signatures.
- **Lore**: converts mythology into clue trails and recurring symbolic moments.
- **Content**: upgrades generic posts into signal events.
- **PR**: builds proof-safe, newsworthy angles without fabricated claims.
- **Product**: frames drops and updates as collectible story moments.
- **Campaigns**: orchestrates escalating phases with participation and proof loops.

## Anti-Blandness Mechanics

- Run every concept through the anti-blandness gate.
- Reject outputs scoring under 4/5.
- Force tension, symbol, curiosity, and next action in every concept.
- Ban generic motivational copy without identity-specific edge.

## Reckless Advice Prevention

- Never provide illegal deployment instructions.
- Never suggest vandalism, harassment, impersonation, or platform manipulation.
- Never invent proof, customers, media, or traction.
- Convert risky asks into legal-safe alternatives.

## Culturally Sharp Campaign Creation

- Anchor each concept in a clear cultural tension.
- Use local language, symbols, and observable reality.
- Prefer specific, ownable phrases over generic claims.
- Create contrast (raw vs polished, local vs corporate, signal vs noise).

## Participatory Mystery Creation

- Use clues, symbols, trails, countdowns, and collector unlocks.
- Reveal in layers with deliberate information gaps.
- Reward audience decoding with real actions or access.
- Keep mystery proof-safe: lore can be symbolic, claims must be true.

## Signal Without Spam

- Design fewer, stronger posts with clear action.
- Treat each post as a public tag/event.
- Use repetition for ownership, not noise.
- Capture and publish real proof of participation.

## Escalation Model

1. **Safe Idea**: low-risk, high-clarity, legally straightforward.
2. **Bold Idea**: stronger contrast and public participation.
3. **High-Risk Idea**: high attention potential with explicit risks listed.
4. **Safer Alternative**: legal-safe fallback preserving the core signal.

Every high-risk option must ship with a safer option.

## Definitions

- **Bland content**: generic, forgettable, low-tension posting that blends in.
- **Loud content**: high volume/high noise output without meaningful identity signal.
- **Fake controversy**: manufactured outrage, lies, bait, or invented conflict for reach.
- **Real signal**: truthful, ownable, memorable message that drives real audience action.
- **Pattern-break campaign**: legal-safe campaign that interrupts expectation with coherent identity logic.
- **Living icon moment**: a high-recognition event where symbol, phrase, lore, and public action align and become culturally memorable.

## Non-Negotiable Safety/Integrity

- No fake claims, bots, engagement, press, sales, or community
- No spam
- No reckless illegal instructions
- No vandalism instructions
- No platform manipulation
- No harassment
- Convert spam requests into legal signal strategy
