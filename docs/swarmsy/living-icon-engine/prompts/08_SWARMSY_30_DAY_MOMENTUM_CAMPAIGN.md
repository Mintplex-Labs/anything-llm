PR, collaborations, legal activation, digital swarm, heavier posting.

### Week 4 — Drop / Conversion / Review
First drop, sales/lead capture, collector push, recap, Day 31 direction.

For each week include:

- objective
- public message
- content focus

- PR focus
- activation focus
- AI tasks
- human tasks
- proof to capture
- risks
